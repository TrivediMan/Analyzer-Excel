📈 Project Overview: Business Performance & Sales AnalyzerThe Business Performance Analyzer is a comprehensive analytical suite (originally developed in Excel) designed to dissect retail data and provide high-level business intelligence. By evaluating sales, profit margins, and customer behavior, it transforms raw transactional records into a multi-layered dashboard for strategic decision-making.✨ Key Analytical ModulesExecutive Dashboard: A centralized summary of core KPIs, including Total Sales ($14,800.48$), Total Profit ($6,090.48$), and Average Discount rates.Customer Intelligence:Top 10 Analysis: Ranks high-value customers (e.g., James Wilson and Mary Davis) by total spend.Segmentation: Tracks customer behavior across different regions and product categories.Advanced "What-If" Analysis: A predictive tool that simulates how varying discount levels (from 1% to 20%) impact overall profitability.Statistical Deep-Dive:Calculates Mean, Median, Mode, and Standard Deviation for all major financial metrics.Includes Standard Error and Sample Variance to measure data reliability.Regional & Category Performance:Compares Sales vs. Profit across North, South, East, and West regions.Visualizes category-specific trends (e.g., Clothing, Electronics, Books, Furniture).Regression & Trend Analysis: Features a "Profit Check By Sales" module with Slope and Intercept calculations to predict future financial outcomes based on current sales volume.🛠️ Technical SpecificationsFormat: Excel Workbook (.xlsx) with specialized CSV exports for data portability.Data Points: Customer ID, Product Category, Sales, Quantity, Discount, Profit, and Timestamps.Logic Implemented: Linear regression, conditional ranking, what-if parameters, and chronological month-over-month comparisons.
